How to Play

Controls

  • X/J are A.
  • Z/Y/Q are B.
  • Shift is Select.
  • Enter is Start.
  • The arrow keys are the d-pad.
Click on the emulator screen to give it keyboard focus and click out of the screen to remove focus. If you have trouble playing the games in your browser, you can also download them to your computer and play them using a dedicated emulator like Mednafen. You can view the source code of the games by visiting its Github page.

Credits
